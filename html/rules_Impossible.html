This is a best out of 5 game mode, if you get 3 wins a point will be added on the scoreboard on the top right. The rules of the game are Fire beats wind but loses to water Wind beats water loses to fire and Water beats fire loses to wind, you choose your element first then it shows what element the computer has chosen. This game mode you have a 0% chance of winning or getting a tie and 100% chance of losing. There is no limit on how many games you can play.
